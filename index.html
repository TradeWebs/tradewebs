Common questions

Straight answers.

No jargon. If you have a question that isn't here, just ask.

Can I speak to someone on the phone before getting started?

Yes, absolutely! While most of our tradesmen prefer signing up instantly online or texting between jobs, we know sometimes it's easier to talk. You can click here to message or call us directly on WhatsApp, or drop us a text and your dedicated manager will call you right back!

Do I own my website?

On our Annual Plan, yes - 100%! You own every line of code from Day 1. If you ever leave, we hand over all your website files and transfer your domain to you free of charge. On our Monthly Plan, you own your domain name, logo, and photos, while the website code is licensed to you as part of our ongoing managed service.

How do I send updates, and what's included?

However's easiest - WhatsApp, email or a call! Send a quick text or voice note and we'll sort it within 24 hours, no forms or support portals. Covers anything content-related: phone numbers, prices, services, swapping or adding photos. Brand new pages or a full redesign aren't included in that - those are a simple £49 add-on.

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime on the monthly plan - no penalty, no awkward conversations. Annual plans don't carry refunds but we'll always try to find something that works.

Will my website show up on Google?

Every site is submitted to Google Search Console and built with local SEO from day one. Results take a few weeks, but we set everything up correctly so the site can rank. The Google Business Profile add-on speeds this up considerably.

What is a Google Business Profile (GBP)?

It's the map listing that appears at the very top of Google when local homeowners search for tradesmen (e.g., 'Emergency Plumber Bristol' or 'Electrician near me'). It is arguably the single most powerful tool for getting local phone calls and high-converting enquiries.

Why do I need your £49 GBP Setup add-on?

Simply having a profile isn't enough to rank in Google's top 3 map results (known as the 'Google 3-Pack'). Our £49 GBP add-on is a full done-for-you service: we claim and verify your profile, write SEO-optimised service descriptions, set up your precise geographical coverage areas, upload your trade photos, and link it directly to your new TradeWebs site to maximise your local map authority and get your phone ringing faster.

How do Google reviews connect to my website?

Customer reviews are the #1 trust factor for homeowners deciding which tradesman to call. When we set up your Google Business Profile, we provide you with a simple 1-click review link to send to happy customers on WhatsApp. As your 5-star reviews grow on Google Maps, they reinforce the credibility of your TradeWebs site, turning more website visitors into booked jobs.

How fast will my site load?

Very fast. We build lightweight static sites - no bloated WordPress themes, no unnecessary plugins. Fast sites also rank better on Google, which is a bonus.
