Common questions

Straight answers.

No jargon. If you have a question that isn't here, just ask.

Do I own my website?

On our Annual Plan, yes — 100%! You own every line of code from Day 1. If you ever leave, we hand over all your website files and transfer your domain to you free of charge. On our Monthly Plan, you own your domain name, logo, and photos, while the website code is licensed to you as part of our ongoing managed service.

How do I send you updates?

However's easiest — WhatsApp, email or a call. Most of our clients just send a message and we sort it within 24 hours. No forms, no portals.

What counts as an update?

Anything related to your content — changing phone numbers or email addresses, updating your prices or list of services, swapping out photos, or adding new project pictures to your gallery. All included, no hourly fees. What isn't included? Building brand new custom pages or complete website redesigns (we offer extra pages as a simple £49 add-on!).

What if I want to cancel?

Cancel anytime on the monthly plan — no penalty, no awkward conversations. Annual plans don't carry refunds but we'll always try to find something that works.

Will my website show up on Google?

Every site is submitted to Google Search Console and built with local SEO from day one. Results take a few weeks, but we set everything up correctly so the site can rank. The Google Business Profile add-on speeds this up considerably.

What's a Google Business Profile?

It's the listing that shows on Google Maps when someone nearby searches for a tradesman. It's free to have and one of the most powerful ways to get local work. We set it up for £49 as a one-off add-on.

How fast will my site load?

Very fast. We build lightweight static sites — no bloated WordPress themes, no unnecessary plugins. Fast sites also rank better on Google, which is a bonus.

Prefer to message?

Most tradesmen find it easier to just drop us a WhatsApp. We reply within a few hours — usually much faster.
